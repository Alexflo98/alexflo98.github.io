ForgeFit Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

ForgeFit (“ForgeFit,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and disclose information when you use the ForgeFit mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide directly in the app, including:

  • Username and profile information
  • Workout templates, workout logs, plans, exercise notes, and session details
  • Nutrition logs, meal data, food plans, hydration logs, and calorie targets
  • Body metrics such as height, weight, goal weight, and related progress data
  • Settings and preferences

b. Sign in with Apple

If you sign in using Sign in with Apple, we may receive limited account information from Apple, such as a stable user identifier, your name (if provided), and an email address or private relay address, depending on your Apple settings.

c. Health Data

If you choose to connect Apple Health, ForgeFit may read and, where you authorize it, write selected Health data such as steps, active energy, hydration, and body weight. Health data is only accessed after you explicitly grant permission.

d. Oura Data

If ForgeFit offers Oura integration and you choose to connect your Oura account, ForgeFit may access selected Oura data that you authorize, such as readiness, activity, sleep, recovery, heart rate, and related wellness metrics. We will only access the scopes and data categories you explicitly authorize through Oura.

e. Nutrition Search and Barcode Data

When you use nutrition search or barcode scan features, ForgeFit may send your search terms or scanned barcode values to our nutrition service providers in order to retrieve food details and nutritional information.

f. Device and Diagnostic Information

We may collect limited technical information needed to operate the app, such as app version, device type, and basic error information. We do not use this information to build advertising profiles.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide and operate ForgeFit features
  • Store and display your workouts, nutrition logs, hydration, and profile data
  • Sync or back up your data when you sign in and enable cloud-linked functionality
  • Provide Apple Health and Oura integrations that you request
  • Retrieve nutrition information and barcode lookup results
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and security
  • Respond to support or privacy requests

3. How Your Data Is Stored

ForgeFit is designed as an offline-first app. Most app data is stored locally on your device. If you sign in, certain account-linked data and backup data may also be stored using cloud infrastructure we control or services we use on your behalf.

Depending on the features you use, data may be stored:

  • Locally on your iPhone or iPad using on-device storage
  • In Firebase services for account-related functions and backup/restore functionality
  • In Apple Health, if you authorize Health write access
  • In Oura-connected systems only as necessary to authorize and retrieve your requested Oura data

4. Third-Party Services

ForgeFit may use third-party services, including:

  • Apple, including Sign in with Apple and Apple Health
  • Firebase, for authentication, cloud functions, and backup-related services
  • FatSecret, for nutrition and food lookup services
  • Oura, if you choose to connect your Oura account

These third parties have their own privacy practices and policies. Your use of connected third-party services may also be governed by their terms.

5. Oura Integration

If you connect Oura to ForgeFit, ForgeFit will access only the Oura data you authorize. We use Oura data solely to provide the wellness, recovery, readiness, sleep, or activity features requested by you inside ForgeFit.

You may revoke Oura access at any time by disconnecting the integration within ForgeFit or through Oura account controls, where available.

6. Apple Health Integration

Health data is only accessed with your permission. Health data is not used for advertising, marketing profiling, or sold to third parties. If you revoke Apple Health permissions in iOS settings, ForgeFit will no longer be able to read or write the affected data types.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information only as necessary to:

  • Provide the app’s requested functionality
  • Operate integrations you choose to enable
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the security, rights, or safety of ForgeFit, our users, or others

8. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to provide the app and related services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Data stored locally remains on your device until you delete the app or remove data in the app. Cloud backup data may remain until deleted or replaced, subject to our operational retention practices.

9. Your Choices

You may:

  • Use ForgeFit without connecting Apple Health or Oura
  • Revoke Apple Health permissions in iOS settings
  • Disconnect Oura integration if enabled
  • Delete app content locally in the app where supported
  • Stop using the app at any time

10. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect data. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Children’s Privacy

ForgeFit is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and address the issue.

12. Medical and Wellness Disclaimer

ForgeFit is a fitness and wellness app and is not a medical device. Content, metrics, recommendations, and integrations are provided for informational and personal wellness purposes only and are not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

13. International Use

If you use ForgeFit outside the United States, you understand that your information may be processed and stored in countries where our service providers operate.

14. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we may update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
