Pump Scout Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01/13/2026

1) Information We Collect

  • Search inputs (city, state, ZIP)
  • Location (if you tap the location button and grant permission)
  • App usage and feature interactions
  • Account identifiers (Sign in with Apple)
  • Email address (only if you choose to share it via Apple)
  • Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID/IDFA if you allow tracking)
  • App diagnostics (crash logs and performance data)

2) Location

If you choose to use the location button, we request "When In Use" location access to determine a nearby ZIP code or city/state. We do not store your precise GPS location. We only use the derived ZIP or city/state to run a search.

3) How We Use Data

  • Provide search results and app features
  • Authenticate your account and link data across sessions
  • Enforce limits and ad rewards
  • Improve reliability and performance
  • Show relevant ads if you allow tracking

4) Sharing

We use third-party services to operate the App:

  • Firebase (authentication, functions, data storage, crash reporting)
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple for authentication)
  • Google AdMob (ads)
  • Apify (data retrieval)

We do not sell personal data.

5) Payments

Subscriptions are handled by Apple. We do not receive or store payment information.

6) Retention

We retain data as needed to provide the service and for analytics. You can clear recent searches, favorites, and history in Settings. You can also delete your account from Settings, which permanently deletes your account and associated data.

7) Your Choices

  • iOS Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Location Services (manage location access)
  • iOS Settings -> Privacy & Security -> Tracking (limit ad tracking)
  • App Store -> Subscriptions (manage Eagle Scout)
  • Settings in the App (clear stored data)
  • Settings -> Account -> Delete Account (permanently delete your account and associated data)

8) Children

The App is not directed to children under 13.

9) Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use means acceptance of the updated policy.
